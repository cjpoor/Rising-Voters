Quick & Easy Registration

We encourage adolescents to pre-register to vote and let your voice be heard. After the significant drop of young voters from 2012 to 2016, turnout has been steadily increasing and we hope to continue this trend.


Steps to Registration

Gather your materials. You will need to know your:

  • Citizenship status
  • Marital status
  • Driver license number
  • Social security number
  • Basic information about yourself

If you are from California, click this button and register to vote! If you are not a California resident, visit the website of your Secretary of State.

Preregister to Vote!

Registration Deadline

In California, you must register to vote for the election by 11:59:59 p.m. Pacific Time on the 15th calendar day before the election.

If the deadline is missed, you can still register for future elections. Wait at least 24 hours for your updated registration status.

Additional Information

For more information on registering to vote you may want to visit the state's Frequently Asked Questions.
