Why pre-register?

Engaging prospective voters through pre-registration programs increases future voting turnout and civic engagement. You must be at least 16 years old to pre-register. Depending on your state, if you are currently 17 years old and will be 18 years old by Election Day, you are permitted to vote in the presidential primary. When to vote

The California primary is on March 3, 2020. This is also when the state elections are. If you are at least 18 years old on that day, you are permitted to participate in the primary and state voting. The general election is on November 3, 2020. If you are 18 by this date, you may vote in the election.

Why vote?

Increased voter turnout increases access to elected officials and gives citizens more power in decisions that affect their country. It is important to remember, every vote matters. Once you have registered, voting when you become eligible ensures that your voice is heard and that you are impacting the policies that will affect your community.
